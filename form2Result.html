About

Farm Aid is a website that utilizes various machine learning models to aid farmers in their harvesting process. This website provides valuable information to farmers regarding which crop to grow, which fertilizer to use, and what diseases the crops may have.

Disease Prediction

The AI model you're referring to is a sophisticated image classification system specifically designed to analyze images of plant leaves. This model employs advanced machine learning techniques to accurately identify and predict diseases affecting plants based on their leaf appearance. It has been trained on a diverse dataset consisting of images representing various diseases and healthy states across 38 different plant classes. When presented with an image of a plant leaf, the AI model utilizes its learned knowledge to assign it to one of the 38 classes. These classes encompass a range of plant species, including apples, blueberries, cherries, corn, grapes, oranges, peaches, pepper bells, potatoes, raspberries, soybeans, squash, strawberries, and tomatoes.
