About

Farm Aid is a website that utilizes various machine learning models to aid farmers in their harvesting process. This website provides valuable information to farmers regarding which crop to grow, which fertilizer to use, and what diseases the crops may have.

FarmAid

Farm Aid is motivated by a desire to support farmers and promote sustainable agriculture practices. By utilizing machine learning algorithms and data analysis, these websites can provide farmers with valuable insights and recommendations to optimize their crop yield and profitability while minimizing environmental impact.

Crop Recommendation
Farm Aid analyzes various factors such as soil quality and weather conditions to suggest the best crop for a particular area.
Disease Prediction
Farmers can now easily submit an image of a diseased leaf and receive an accurate prediction of which disease the plant is likely to have.
Fertilizer Reccomendation
Farm Aid also provides valuable insights into fertilization, analyzing soil quality data to recommend the best fertilizer.

Motive

Farm Aid's use of machine learning algorithms provides farmers with valuable insights and recommendations to optimize their crop yield and profitability. By leveraging the power of data analysis and machine learning, Farm Aid is helping farmers to make informed decisions about their farming practices and improve their crop yield.
