About

Farm Aid is a website that utilizes various machine learning models to aid farmers in their harvesting process. This website provides valuable information to farmers regarding which crop to grow, which fertilizer to use, and what diseases the crops may have.

Crop Recommendation

Our crop recommendation model is an intelligent system that assists farmers in making informed decisions about which crops to cultivate based on various soil properties and environmental factors. This model takes into account essential parameters such as pH level, nitrogen content, phosphorous content, potassium content, humidity, and temperature to provide accurate crop recommendations.
